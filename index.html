About Me

Yiran Wang is currently pursuing a PhD degree in Computer Science at Linköping University within the Wallenberg AI, Autonomous Systems, and Software Program (WASP). She received her Master’s degree in Statistics and Machine Learning from Linköping University. Her research focuses on the quality of machine learning code, using software engineering techniques and large language models, with a particular emphasis on machine learning applications developed in Jupyter notebooks.
