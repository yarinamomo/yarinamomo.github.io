About Me

Yiran Wang received her Master’s degree in Statistics and Machine Learning from Linköping University, where she is currently pursuing a PhD degree in Computer Science within the Wallenberg AI, Autonomous Systems, and Software Program (WASP). Her research focuses on the quality of machine learning code, using software engineering techniques and large language models, with a particular emphasis on machine learning applications developed in Jupyter notebooks.
